# Code of Conduct for Financial Swarm Project

## Our Principles

We are committed to creating a positive, inclusive, and professional environment for all contributors. Our project values diversity, respectful collaboration, and ethical conduct.

## Expected Behavior

- Show respect for all participants
- Be constructive in feedback and criticism
- Communicate professionally and clearly
- Credit contributions appropriately
- Help create a welcoming environment

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Personal attacks or insults
- Disruptive or offensive behavior
- Sharing private information without consent
- Any form of professional misconduct

## Reporting and Enforcement

Reports of unacceptable behavior should be sent to the project maintainer at zhichao.pan@example.com. All reports will be handled confidentially and addressed promptly.

Consequences for violations may include:
- Private warnings
- Temporary project restrictions
- Permanent removal from the community

## Professional Standards

As a project involving financial analysis:
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive data
- Follow ethical guidelines for AI development
- Ensure compliance with relevant regulations
- Disclose limitations and assumptions
